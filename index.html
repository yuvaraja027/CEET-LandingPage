Centre For Excellence in Energy and Telecommunication

Centre for Excellence in Energy and Telecommunication (CEET) is a non-profit R&D organization backed by IIT Madras. CEET specializes in the research and development of complete lifecycle of battery subsystems including battery packs, motors and controllers, charging subsystems and storage/grid batteries.

Energy Efficiency

Efficient management of demand and supply between available renewable and grid power sources.

E-Mobility

Complete design and development of E-mobility subsystems.

Motors & Controllers

Electric Vehicle (2W/3W/4W/LCV) Motors and Controller development.

Large Scale Energy Storage System/MicroGrid

Cutting-edge research on renewable energy integration, smart grid technologies, and microgrid development.

Battery and Solar Panel Recycling

Battery and Solar Panel Recycling facilities to ensure sustainability.
